Dr. Anshuman Gahlot MBBS, MS (OPHTHALMOLOGY), FVRS Dr Anshuman Gehlot at MeeraSight Dr. Anshuman Gahlot MS, FVRS is a specialist in Vitreoretinal surgery who has MS Ophthalmology from Manipur University followed by long-term Fellowship in Vitreoretinal Surgery from Chaithanya Institute of Ophthalmology and Visual Sciences, Trivandrum.
