Catract

Cataract is the clouding of the eye's natural lens. It is the most common cause of vision loss in people over age 40 and is also the principal cause of blindness in the world. Cataract can blur your vision making it difficult to see near as well as distance objects. ​ Cataract is treated surgically by Advanced Cataract surgery also known as Phacoemulsification/MICS is a simple, relatively painless procedure to regain vision. During surgery, we will remove your clouded lens through a very small cut (1.2mm-2.8mm) and in most cases replace it with a clear, plastic intraocular lens (IOL). ​ MeeraSight is equipped with the latest ophthalmic equipment and most modern operation theatre to diagnose and treat your cataract safely and efficiently.
