Comprehensive Eye Examination

The eyes are among the most sensitive organs of the body and are considered to be one of the most important senses. Unlike other parts of our bodies, our eyes don’t always tend to hurt very much when there is something wrong. ​ Regular eye health exams will not just ensure that you are able to see as best as possible but also check for signs of eye disease or conditions that can affect not only your vision but your overall health. ​ At MeeraSight we have 2 consultation rooms which are fully equipped to conduct a comprehensive examination of every patient which includes: A detailed history taking to know the risk factors of different diseases Visual acuity test Examination for eye alignment, eye pressure Slit lamp examination for early signs of eye diseases A dilated retina checkup. Specialized tests if considered necessary. ​ KNOW MORE about the benefits, frequency and importance of regular and Comprehensive eye checkups.
